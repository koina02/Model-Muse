About Naomi

Commanding presence meets effortless elegance - discover the woman behind the muse

The Essence of Modern Elegance

Naomi Elle is a Kenyan-born fashion model whose striking features and versatile talent have graced international runways, editorial spreads, and advertising campaigns worldwide. With roots in Nairobi's vibrant fashion scene, Naomi brings a unique global perspective to every project.

Her journey began at 18 when she was discovered during a local fashion week. Since then, Naomi has worked with prestigious brands including Louis Vuitton, Dior, and Valentino, as well as emerging African designers pushing the boundaries of contemporary fashion.
