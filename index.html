About Naomi

With a presence that commands attention and versatility that transcends categories, Naomi brings a unique perspective to every project.

The Essence of Modern Elegance

Naomi Elle is a London-based model known for her striking features and ability to transform for any creative vision. With roots in both European and African heritage, she brings a unique global perspective to her work.

Beginning her career at 18, Naomi has since worked with prestigious brands like Chanel, Dior, and Valentino, as well as emerging designers pushing the boundaries of fashion.
